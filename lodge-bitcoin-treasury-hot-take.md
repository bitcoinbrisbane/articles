# The government just shut down Texas's biggest poker room with zero charges. This is why Bitcoin exists.

I was literally just at The Lodge. Played cards. Had a great time. Weeks later, [20 TABC agents storm the place](https://www.pokernews.com/news/2026/03/lodge-card-club-texas-poker-room-raided-50786.htm), players are told to pocket their chips and leave, and the doors get locked.

Charges filed? [Zero.](https://www.casino.org/news/money-laundering-illegal-gambling-probe-behind-raid-on-austins-the-lodge-card-club/)
Arrests made? Zero.
Explanation given to the owners? [None.](https://www.pokernews.com/news/2026/03/lodge-card-club-poker-room-raid-statement-50794.htm)

Doug Polk is out here calling it a ["witch hunt"](https://www.pokernews.com/news/2026/03/doug-polk-address-lodge-poker-raid-50793.htm) and personally guaranteeing player funds — because apparently in America, you can have your business seized and you, the owner, have to scramble to make customers whole out of your own pocket.

This isn't some underground gambling den. This is a 70-table poker room that hosts [WPT events](https://www.gamblinginsider.com/news/117311/wpt-postpones-lodge-event-texas-raid). And the government just shut it down on suspicion alone.

Civil asset forfeiture is [one of the most criticised practices in American law](https://harvardlawreview.org/print/vol-131/how-crime-pays-the-unconstitutionality-of-modern-civil-asset-forfeiture-as-a-tool-of-criminal-law-enforcement/). The government takes your stuff, charges your *property* with a crime (not you), and then *you* have to prove it's innocent. It's guilty-until-proven-innocent for your bank account.

You know what can't be frozen by the TABC? Bitcoin in a hardware wallet.

This is not a hypothetical. This is not a thought experiment. This is a real business, run by real people, shut down with real consequences — and zero due process.

[170+ public companies](https://coinmarketcap.com/charts/bitcoin-treasuries/) already hold Bitcoin on their balance sheet. Strategy holds [717,000+ BTC](https://www.demandsage.com/public-companies-holding-btc/). They get it. If your money is in a bank, it's not really your money — it's money the government lets you use until they decide otherwise.

Not your keys, not your coins. Not your keys, not your business.
